One of the most researched topics is the new cutting-edge technology of DLTs.

Over the last few years, the use of Blockchain and other DLTs has seen tremendous growth. Their use has been suggested for various applications since they can provide transparency and accountability. However, Blockchain and other DLTs are highly susceptible to quantum attacks. Quantum computers present a threat to many cryptographic protocols that exist today.
